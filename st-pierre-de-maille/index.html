Molante Farm      

Ferme De Molante, St Pierre De Maille, France

Molante Farm is a B&B located 500 metres from the village of Saint-Pierre-de-Maillé. Free Wi-Fi access is available, as well as a garden with terrace and BBQ facilities. The bedrooms have a private bathroom with shower and hairdryer, a private toilet, a wardrobe and a desk. Served daily, the continental breakfast includes pastries, bread, jam, juice, coffee, tea, chocolate and fresh fruit. Guests can also use a shared kitchen. At Molante Farm you will find a garden, barbecue facilities and a terrace. Other facilities offered at the property include a TV room and luggage storage. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including and cycling hiking. La Roche Posay is 12 km from the B&B, while St Savin Cathedral is 14 km away and there is free parking is available on site.
