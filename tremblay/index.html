Situated Tremblay, this property provides self-catering accommodation with free private parking, free Wi-Fi internet access and an outdoor swimming pool. Fougères is 10 km away. With a private terrace, each cottage/house features a fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher, washing machine, electric kettle and dining area. A game room is at children's disposal and the living room has a fireplace, LCD TV and DVD player. The garden is fitted with parasols, furniture and barbecue facilities. Mont Saint Michel is 30 km away and Saint Malo is about 1 hour's drive away. The A84 motorway is 10 km away and Rennes Train Station is a 45-minute drive away.
